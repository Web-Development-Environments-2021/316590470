History

My name is Farcha but everyone is calling me Nana! I was born in Iraq in the 20th century, got married when I was only 18 years old, and immigrated to Israel with my children. I hava 5 children - 4 boys and 1 girl, up to 10 grandchildren, and at least 5 great-grandchildren

Background

My family and I built up a knitting factory called "Elinit", back then we were selling quality sweaters to many clothing brand stores in Israel, until one day everyone started to buy not so quality cloths from China which caused unfortunately the closing of our dearly company.

Hobbies

I spent most of the time at home, cooking, watching tv and knitting sweaters, domes, scraves and so on. I very much like hosting and having guests, every saturday my children and their own children come visiting me, playing domino and telling me stories.

Nana's Everyday

As old people we have a weekly club appointment which we all sit together, play social games and talk a lot.

Inspirational

My husband was my inspirational character for life. He was the love of my life and unfortunately I have lost him at such a young age.
